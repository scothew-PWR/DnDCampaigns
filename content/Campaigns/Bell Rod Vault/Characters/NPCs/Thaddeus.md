## Overview

Thaddeus is one of the most powerful, enigmatic, and dangerous entities connected to the Bell Rod Vault.

At first glance, he can appear as a strange, eccentric old wizard—rambling, unpredictable, and borderline insane.

That impression is dangerously incomplete.

Thaddeus is not merely a wizard.

He appears to be a vessel containing multiple powerful souls or personas, each with distinct identities, motives, and temperaments.

His true nature remains one of the campaign’s greatest mysteries.

## Appearance

Thaddeus most commonly manifests as an elderly hooded wizard.

Known features:

- aged appearance
    
- robes / cloak
    
- ancient presence
    
- obscured features
    
- unsettling aura of hidden power
    

He carries the energy of someone who has lived far too long and seen far too much.

Even when calm, he feels dangerous.

## Personality

Trying to define Thaddeus as a single personality is nearly impossible.

Depending on which persona is dominant, he can appear:

- whimsical
    
- cryptic
    
- benevolent
    
- manipulative
    
- terrifying
    
- detached
    

He often behaves unpredictably, making him difficult to read.

It is unclear whether this unpredictability is:

- madness
    
- deliberate deception
    
- internal conflict between souls
    
- or all of the above

## Known History

Thaddeus has repeatedly appeared near major campaign turning points.

He seems deeply tied to:

- the vault
    
- celestial conflicts
    
- coin mechanics
    
- soul manipulation
    

He possesses knowledge far beyond what he willingly shares.

### Gork Connection

Before the party entered the vault, Thaddeus sent [[Gork]] to assist them.

At some point, Gork disappeared.

Later, the party encountered Gork again inside the dungeon city, serving as Thaddeus’s executioner.

This raised major questions about Thaddeus’s true intentions.

### City Encounter

The party observed Thaddeus alongside Gork in the dungeon city where [[Dilbert]] was found.

This moment reinforced his role as a major power player within the vault.

## Magical Capabilities

Observed or inferred capabilities include:

- soul manipulation
    
- soul binding
    
- reality bending
    
- advanced arcana
    
- knowledge of vault systems
    
- deep coin awareness
    

The fact that he could bind a soul to a weapon suggests terrifying magical mastery.

## Threat Assessment

**Threat Level: Extreme**

If Thaddeus ever becomes openly hostile, he may represent one of the most dangerous beings the party has encountered.

His greatest danger may not be raw power alone.

It may be his understanding of the deeper rules governing:

- souls
    
- coins
    
- the vault
    
- celestial punishment
