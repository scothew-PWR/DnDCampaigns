## Overview

Zava is the party’s barbarian and the physical embodiment of overwhelming force.

When subtlety fails—and in this party, that happens often—Zava becomes the answer.

He is a frontline shock trooper built around aggression, durability, and relentless pressure. Once committed to a target, Zava becomes incredibly difficult to stop. Despite his brutal combat style, Zava is more than just muscle. Beneath the barbarian rage is someone surprisingly disciplined, emotionally honest, and deeply committed to strength, health, and personal standards.

He is equal parts:

- warrior
    
- athlete
    
- tank
    
- battering ram
    

With occasional himbo energy.

## Appearance

Zava carries the unmistakable presence of a veteran warrior built for physical domination.

Known traits:

- powerful muscular build
    
- intimidating presence
    
- athletic conditioning
    
- battlefield confidence
    
- relentless physical energy

He looks like someone who takes physical conditioning seriously. And he does.

## Personality

Core traits:

- fearless
    
- direct
    
- aggressive
    
- emotionally honest
    
- disciplined
    
- loyal
    

Zava is straightforward in a way many others are not. He generally says what he means. You rarely need to guess where you stand with him.

He values:

- strength
    
- discipline
    
- resilience
    
- action over hesitation

While often associated with rage and violence, Zava is not reckless. He believes in preparation. He respects effort. He appreciates people who push themselves.

## Health and Fitness

One of Zava’s defining non-combat traits is his commitment to health and physical performance.

He places high value on:

- physical training
    
- conditioning
    
- nutrition
    
- wellness
    
- bodily discipline

Zava approaches his body like a weapon. Maintenance matters. Strength matters. Performance matters. This makes him unusually health-conscious for a stereotypical barbarian. He is not simply strong by accident. He works at it. Constantly.

## Pie

Despite his strong focus on health and discipline…

Zava absolutely loves pie. This has become one of his more charming contradictions. For someone so committed to fitness, his enthusiasm for pie is legendary. Apparently even elite barbarian discipline has exceptions. Pie is one of them.

Whether justified as:

- cheat meals
    
- carb loading
    
- morale boosting
    
- emotional support dessert

…depends entirely on who is asking.

## Combat Role

Primary role:

- tank
    
- shock assault
    
- brute force
    
- frontline pressure
    

Zava thrives in close-range combat.

His specialties include:

- absorbing punishment
    
- forcing enemy attention
    
- disrupting enemy formations
    
- creating chaos in melee
    

When Zava commits, enemy plans tend to change fast.

## Memorable Moments

### Claude Charge

During the Four Champions battle, Zava aggressively charged [[Claude the Clown]] alone.

This was classic Zava.

Identify threat.  
Close distance.  
Apply violence.

Unfortunately, this exposed him to severe punishment from:

- [[Claude the Clown]]
    
- [[Roland the Rejected]]
    

He took heavy damage but survived.

The play embodied everything about him:

Aggressive. Fearless. Committed.

---

### Surviving Focus Fire

Despite taking concentrated punishment from multiple champions, Zava stayed standing.

His durability helped prevent the fight from collapsing.

He absorbed incredible pressure.

---

### Celestial Titan Incident

One of the funniest moments in the campaign involved Zava’s reaction to the [[Celestial Titan]].

Despite being known for fearless aggression and immense physical confidence…

Zava became visibly infatuated. The reaction was immediate and obvious.

This became even funnier because:

**Zava is married.**

The contrast between:

- unstoppable barbarian  
    
    and
    
- suddenly flustered married man around divine muscle-mommy energy

made the moment unforgettable. This remains one of the party’s greatest running jokes.
