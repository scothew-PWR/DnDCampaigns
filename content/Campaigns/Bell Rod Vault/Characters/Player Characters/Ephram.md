## Overview

Ephram is the party’s cleric and primary support specialist. He provides healing, restoration, utility, and clutch battlefield manipulation.

While often less flashy than the frontliners, Ephram has repeatedly saved encounters through clever utility and timely support.

## Appearance

- Cleric attire / divine gear
    
- Support-oriented equipment
    
- Often positioned behind frontline allies
    

## Personality

Core traits:

- supportive
    
- clever
    
- adaptable
    
- resilient
    

Ephram often succeeds by thinking creatively under pressure.

He is far more dangerous than enemies tend to assume.

## Known History

Ephram has repeatedly proven capable of turning losing situations into manageable ones through control and support magic.

He serves as one of the party’s most reliable anchors during chaotic encounters.

## Combat Role

Primary role:

- healing
    
- buffs
    
- debuffs
    
- support control
    
- utility
    

Strengths:

- sustain
    
- status recovery
    
- tactical spell usage
    

## Relationships

### [[Mordikahn]]

Ephram has demonstrated strong teamwork with Mordikahn.

Their most iconic moment involved an intimidation combo in the guild hall.

## Memorable Moments

### Shoulder Intimidation

During an early guild hall encounter, Ephram climbed onto [[Mordikahn]]’s shoulders to intimidate a suspicious figure.

That figure was later revealed to be [[Roland the Rejected]].

An absurd but unforgettable moment.

### Vanguard #7 Charm

During the Four Champions battle, Ephram landed a critical charm effect on [[Vanguard 7]].

This shifted the encounter dramatically.

Instead of fighting the construct alone, the party gained temporary assistance.

This may have prevented disaster.

### Blind and Deaf Survival

During Mind Sweepfield, Ephram became:

- blind
    
- deaf
    

Despite this, he continued functioning through clever use of his spider familiar.

That was pure adaptability.
