## Overview

Fnipper is a rogue/warlock defined by chaos, audacity, and impossible luck.

He is a thief, opportunist, gambler, and wildcard.

Underestimate him at your own risk.

## Personality

- sneaky
    
- impulsive
    
- chaotic
    
- clever
    
- dangerous
    

Fnipper thrives on risk.

## Known History

Fnipper possesses a mysterious puzzle orb functioning as a lockbox tied to coin mysteries.

[[Thaddeus]] linked Fnipper’s soul to his bow.

He also formed a pact with [[Fortuna]], the Gambler.

## Coin Bond

Bound to [[Fortuna]].
