## Overview

Zanaris is a Human Ranger and one of the party’s most tactically disciplined combatants. Where others solve problems through force or chaos, Zanaris excels through positioning, precision, and battlefield control.

She frequently enables the party by disrupting enemy plans before they can fully develop.

## Appearance

- Human female
    
- Ranger gear suited for mobility and ranged combat
    
- Carries herself with calm focus
    
- Alert and observant
    

Zanaris projects control rather than intimidation.

## Personality

Core traits:

- calm
    
- practical
    
- perceptive
    
- disciplined
    
- reliable
    

Zanaris tends to avoid unnecessary chaos and often acts as a stabilizing influence when the rest of the party becomes… unpredictable.

She thinks ahead and values tactical advantage.

## Known History

Though less flashy than some party members, Zanaris has repeatedly proven critical during high-threat encounters.

Her ability to read the battlefield and apply pressure at key moments has shifted major fights in the party’s favor.

## Combat Role

Primary role:

- ranged damage
    
- battlefield control
    
- utility support
    
- tactical disruption
    

Strengths:

- positioning
    
- ranged pressure
    
- target denial
    
- mobility
    

She is especially effective against enemy casters.

## Relationships

### Party

Zanaris often functions as one of the more grounded tactical minds in the group.

She balances:

- Mordikahn’s aggression
    
- Fnipper’s chaos
    
- Zava’s brute force
    

## Memorable Moments

### Claude Shutdown

During the Four Champions fight, Zanaris delivered one of the most important plays of the encounter.

She successfully applied **Silence** to [[Claude the Clown]].

This forced Claude into a terrible position:

- spellcasting restricted
    
- movement forced
    
- tactical options reduced
    

That single action dramatically altered the battle.

This was one of the biggest clutch support plays in the campaign.
