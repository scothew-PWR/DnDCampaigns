# Shit Ray Says

# Psychological Warfare

---

> “You think you disabled it.”

**Threat Level:** ☠️☠️☠️☠️☠️

Context:  
The party believes they successfully shut down, disabled, or neutralized something.

Ray disagrees.

Possible meanings:
- It is still active
- There is a backup system
- You triggered phase 2
- You made it angry

Player Effects:
- Confidence: -100  
- Stress: +50  
- Paranoia: +200  

Ray Translation:
> “Your assumption was incorrect, and I’m enjoying this.”

---

> “Are you sure?”

**Threat Level:** ☠️☠️☠️

This is not a question.

This is a warning.

When Ray asks this, he is giving you one final chance to avoid catastrophe.

---

> “Go ahead.”

**Threat Level:** ☠️☠️☠️☠️

He already knows what happens next.

It will not be good.

---

# DM Threats

---

Usually means:
- It probably won’t work
- Failure may be painful
- Ray wants to watch

---

> “Roll me a save.”

Immediate heart rate increase.

No one enjoys hearing this.

---

> “Nothing seems to happen.”

**This is almost certainly a lie.**

Possible real meaning:
- Something invisible happened
- Delayed effect
- Trap armed
- Curse applied
- Initiative soon

---

> “That happens.”

This is Ray’s version of emotional detachment.

Something horrible just occurred.

Ray is calm.

You should not be.
