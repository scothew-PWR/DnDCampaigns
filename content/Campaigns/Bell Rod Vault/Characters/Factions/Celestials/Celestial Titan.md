## Overview

She only has a title, as she is a tool. An example that celestials do not have independence. She said she is called Wrath and Judgment.

The Celestial Titan is an immense divine being whose presence left a powerful impression on the party—especially [[Zava]].

As the name implies, this entity exists on a scale far beyond ordinary mortals, and likely above many other celestial beings as well.

The Titan radiates overwhelming power, divine authority, and awe-inspiring physical presence.

Where some celestials feel ethereal, graceful, or distant…

The Celestial Titan feels primal.

Massive.

Unavoidable.

This is divine power made physical.

The Titan feels less like a person and more like a force of reality.

## Appearance

The Celestial Titan possesses overwhelming physical and divine beauty.

Known characteristics include:

- colossal stature
    
- immense physical build
    
- celestial radiance
    
- divine presence
    
- breathtaking beauty
    
- overwhelming aura of power
    

The Titan’s form conveys impossible strength even while standing still.

Every movement feels deliberate and heavy with meaning.

Its presence dominates everything nearby.

To mortals, standing before such a being would feel almost impossible to process.

The first instinct would likely be one of:

- awe
    
- reverence
    
- fear
    
- admiration
    

Possibly all at once.

## Presence and Aura

The Celestial Titan radiates power so immense that it can be felt before it is fully understood.

Its presence evokes:

- divine majesty
    
- overwhelming pressure
    
- primal strength
    
- ancient authority
    
- impossible beauty
    

Unlike subtle divine entities, the Titan does not feel delicate.

It feels absolute.

Its presence carries the sensation of something ancient and foundational.

Like a mountain that learned how to walk.

## Personality

Very little is currently known about the Titan’s true personality.

Possible observed traits:

- composed
    
- powerful
    
- commanding
    
- ancient
    
- self-assured
    

The Titan carries the presence of someone who has never needed to prove their strength.

It simply exists.

And everyone around it understands.

That level of quiet confidence is almost more intimidating than aggression.

## Known History

Little concrete history is currently known about the Celestial Titan.

However, its existence reinforces an important truth about the campaign’s cosmology:

Not all celestials are equal.

Some divine beings exist on a scale approaching myth.

The Celestial Titan appears to be one of those entities.

Its role within celestial hierarchy remains unknown.

Possible roles include:

- divine guardian
    
- celestial champion
    
- ancient enforcer
    
- primordial being
    

## Role in the Story

The Celestial Titan represents one of the clearest glimpses into the upper tiers of divine existence.

Its presence expands the scale of the campaign dramatically.

The party is no longer merely dealing with:

- monsters
    
- dungeons
    
- champions
    

They are brushing against cosmic powers.

The Titan serves as proof of that.

## Relationship to [[Zava]]

One of the most memorable and hilarious details surrounding the Celestial Titan is its effect on [[Zava]].

Zava became noticeably infatuated with the Titan.

This is especially funny because Zava is typically defined by:

- aggression
    
- directness
    
- brute force confidence
    
- fearless violence
    

Yet in the Titan’s presence…

Even Zava became visibly affected.

Whether this reaction was:

- attraction
    
- admiration
    
- fascination
    
- divine influence
    

remains unclear.

Possibly all of the above.

What makes this even better:

**Zava is married.**

This transformed the moment from amusing to legendary party comedy.

The contrast between:

- unstoppable barbarian energy  
    
    and
    
- suddenly flustered married man around divine muscle mommy energy
    

made the encounter unforgettable.

### Reverence

## Threat Assessment

Potentially catastrophic.

That is probably for the best.
